So who am I?

I am Matt. I am 22 years old and hope to become a software engineer someday! Some information about me that you might find interesting is that I was born and raised in a small beach town called Cape May, NJ. My family has also owned a small business in Cape May for the past 20 years. I have worked in the baking industry since I was 6 years old. If any of you guys visit Cape May you probably have seen me working at one of our bakeries or have seen or heard of our bakery famously known as Madison's Bakery. My girlfriend Mackenzi and I love candles so we created our own candle company called Cape May Candle Company and Beach Barn Candle Company. I also come from a pretty large family. I have 2 brothers, 3 sisters, 2 nieces and 1 nephew.
